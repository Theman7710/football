Chelsea in talks to keep Sancho

Chelsea would like Sancho to stay if they can agree on contract terms which fit in with the wage structure at the club.

Sancho joined Chelsea on loan from Manchester United last August and Chelsea now have a £25 million obligation to buy.

They can pay United a £5 million penalty to get out of the obligation to buy.

Trent Alexander-Arnold

Los Blancos kick-start their Club World Cup campaign against Saudi Pro League giants Al-Hilal on June 18, and the Spanish side want the right-back available for that match.

However, Alexander-Arnold's contract with Liverpool expires on June 30, meaning that Los Blancos will need to negotiate the player out of his terms early.

There is the scope to complete such a move, with FIFA opening a special transfer window between June 1 and June 10 due to the Club World Cup.
