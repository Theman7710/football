Football is a general term for a family of a team of sports that involve kicking a ball to score goals.It's a game of 11side,7side and 5side

The basic rule of football,also known as association football or soccer

focus on advancing the ball down the field using the feet, with a goal of scoring by kicking the ball into the opposing team's goal.

A match is played by two teams, each with a maximum of 11 players, and the objective is to score more goals than the opposing team.
